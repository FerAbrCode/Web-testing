This page explains how to think about breaking news in prediction markets (like Polymarket) and how to turn a headline into a clear, testable market question.

Implications for Polymarket (general)

Breaking news typically moves markets because it changes the probability of an outcome, not because it is “good” or “bad.” The strongest price moves usually happen when news is both credible (verifiable) and surprising (meaningfully different from what traders expected).
